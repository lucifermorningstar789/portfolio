Project 1

This data science project aims to predict the stock market direction (up or down) for the current week using historical data. The dataset includes daily open, close, high, low, and volume for major indices. Features are engineered using moving averages, RSI, MACD, and sentiment analysis from financial news headlines. A classification model such as Random Forest or XGBoost is trained and validated on past weeks to classify the upcoming week's market movement. Accuracy, precision, and confusion matrix are used to evaluate performance, and predictions are visualized alongside confidence scores.
